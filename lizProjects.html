Enhancing Cavite State University (CvSU) University Portal

The main goal of the project is to redesign the Cavite State University portal to enhance its user interface. We want to achieve a modern and aesthetically pleasing design that aligns with the university’s identity. We also want to simplify navigation, and add features that will engage the users. We will also make sure that the portal will be accessible to everyone, including those with disabilities.

This project will be covered to make the user experience an appealing and easy to access university portal. The proponents will redesign the homepage, dashboards and some key sections of administration,services, campus and colleges etc. By making the key sections components layout simpler and easy to navigate. It focuses on reducing user clicks that the proponents experienced to the current university portal. Having enhancements to its mobile responsiveness and fixing new user interface designs like buttons, forms, icons, adding a search bar, and fixing the sections of the contents. Simplified its drop-down menus, needed to access important sections. Making sure that some branding elements, such as colors, logos and fonts are consistent to its university branding. Otherwise this project will not be covered by any changes with the university systems information.

ConversAI: AI-Powered Chatbot for Instant and Accurate General Academic Assistance

The primary objective of this project is to enhance the academic experience for students by developing a chatbot that provides immediate and accurate responses to a wide range of inquiries related to their studies. The project aims to facilitate quicker access to essential information, improve student engagement through natural conversation, and reduce frustration caused by traditional support systems, such as waiting for faculty responses. This AI system will minimize response times by efficiently handling a high volume of student queries simultaneously and providing 24/7 support, ensuring students receive assistance whenever they need it. This chatbot will also automate repetitive tasks and address frequently asked questions about course materials, assignment deadlines, and campus resources, thereby improving overall satisfaction and academic success.
